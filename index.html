Asgard

The dwelling-place of the gods

Legend divided Asgard into 12 or more realms, including Valhalla, the home of Odin and the abode of heroes slain in earthly battle; Thrudheim, the realm of Thor; and Breidablik, the home of Balder.

Alfheim

The land of the elves

Freyr was said to be the ruler of Alfheim.
